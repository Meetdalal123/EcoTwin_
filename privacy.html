Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

1. Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personal information on external database servers. If you sign in using Google, we access only your basic public profile name and avatar photo to personalize your twin profile locally.

2. Local Storage

All your carbon footprint parameters, completed diagnostics responses, XP levels, eco challenges status, time capsule messages, and historical trend scores are saved directly in your web browser's local storage (LocalStorage). This data never leaves your device.

3. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can erase all saved parameters and diagnostic sessions at any time by clicking the "Reset All" or "Reset My Data" buttons in your dashboard profile settings, or by clearing your browser cache/cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

We load Google Identity Services SDK to facilitate Google OAuth Sign-in integration. Please review Google's privacy policy for details regarding their authentication handles.
