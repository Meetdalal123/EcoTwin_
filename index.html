Carbon Footprint 101

What is a Carbon Footprint?

The total greenhouse gas emissions (primarily CO₂ & CH₄) produced directly and indirectly by our lifestyles—covering travel, heating, diets, and consumer shopping.

The Climate Goal

To stabilize global warming below the critical 1.5°C threshold, average individual emissions must decrease from the current global 4 Tons/year down to a sustainable 2 Tons/year by 2050.

Greenhouse Blanket

Emissions trap heat in our outer atmosphere, raising global temperature levels and causing severe droughts or intense storms.

Primary Catalysts

Solo vehicle transportation, non-renewable thermal home heating, heavy red meat consumption, and fast-fashion manufacturing.
